If you're serious about SEO: Semrush. It's the Swiss Army knife of SEO — keyword research, competitor spying, site audits, rank tracking, backlink analysis, AND content optimization. Nothing else comes close as an all-in-one solution.

Pro tip: Use both together. Semrush for research and strategy, Surfer for content optimization. They integrate directly with each other.
